CCVL Research Group, Johns Hopkins University

GitHub · Google Scholar

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science, Johns Hopkins University, advised by Bloomberg Distinguished Professor Alan Yuille. Before joining Johns Hopkins University, I obtained my B.Sc. degree in 2016 from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research interest is computer vision with focuses on image recognition with limited data, object detection, and instance segmentation. I’ve been really fortunate to have worked with great research scientists in YITU, Baidu IDL, Adobe, and Google.

I’ll join Google as a Research Scientist after graduation.
